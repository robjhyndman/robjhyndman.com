I will discuss and demonstrate several digital tools that underpin the efficiency and reproducibility of a modern research workflow. 

## Tools covered

### Citing

 * [Mendeley](http://www.mendeley.com)
 * [Zotero](http://www.zotero.org)
 * [Paperpile](https://paperpile.com/)

### Searching

 * [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com.au)
 * [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org)

### Scripting

* [R](https://r-project.org)
* [RStudio](https://posit.co/download/rstudio-desktop/)
* [renv](https://rstudio.github.io/renv/)

### Saving

 * [GitHub](https://github.com/)
 * [Happy git with R](http://happygitwithr.com)

### Caching

* [targets](https://docs.ropensci.org/targets/)

### Writing

* [Quarto](https://quarto.org)
* [Quarto thesis template](https://github.com/quarto-monash/thesis)
